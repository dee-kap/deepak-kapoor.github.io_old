Managing Startups Best Blog Posts - Book Review

May 29th, 2013

Managing Startups: Best Blog Posts by Tom Eisenmann is is a collection of blog posts about management of technology startups. There are a total of 75 posts which provide with valuable guidance for existing entrepreneurs and those who are at initial stages of creating a startup.

When I started reading this book, in my mind I started pretending that I am about to launch a startup which builds an online product targeting a niche market. I am sure that one day such a thing will happen. Reading the book as a startup founder gave me appreciation towards the practical information captured in the collected posts. There are posts which I would like to read again and keep them in my knowledge base.

I suggest that you read it once and then re-read the posts you like most.
