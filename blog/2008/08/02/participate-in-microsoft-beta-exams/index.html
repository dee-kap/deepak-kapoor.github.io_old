Participate in Microsoft Beta Exams

Aug 2nd, 2008

Before a certification exam goes live it is released as beta. Beta exams are by invite only and there are limited number of places. These exams also run for a short period of time. Till now finding about beta exams has been a bit of a trick. Your best bets are forums or various blogs. However this is changing. There is a new blog called Beta Exam Announcements where you can find out about upcoming beta exams. If you are interested in finding about beta exams then subscribe to the feed of Beta Exam Announcements.
