I downloaded Cloudera CDH3 Virtual Machine for VirtualBox to have a readymade Hadoop environment to experiment with. While booting the VM I got an error and the boot sequence stalled.

 

The issue here is that IOAPIC is disabled for this VM. You can find out more about IOAPIC here.

To enable IOAPIC, power off your virtual machine. Go to settings â€“> System. Click on Motherboard tab and check Enable IO APIC.

After this Cloudera image boots fine.
