At home I connect to my wireless network. Recently I faced a problem with my new laptop. The issue was that I could not connect to wireless network at home. After many attempts to repair, renew my IP address and apply other tricks I decided to uninstall Vodafone software. After uninstalling Vodafone software I could connect to Wi-Fi. But I need to connect to mobile internet when I am on road. Thankfully I found the solution to my problem

Solution

Ditch the Vodafone software and start using MDMA (Mobile Data Monitoring Application). MDMA does not require you to install anything and works without any problems. I can now connect to mobile Internet and to Wi-Fi without any fuss. MDMA is also very light weight and has a small memory footprint. You can download MDMA here.
