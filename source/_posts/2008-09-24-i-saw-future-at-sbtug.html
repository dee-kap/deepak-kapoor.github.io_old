I have always found the presentations at SBTUG to be informative and more importantly entertaining. August is a great presenter and I felt that his presentation was well structured and very well rehearsed. All I have to say about the video is that I want all that stuff now. Due to a previously planned family commitment I missed the second presentation. Sorry Mitch.
