Microsoft is offering free website hosting and domain of your choice when you sign up for Office Live Small Business. Included in the package are hosting, your own domain if it is available, and online applications like Contact Manager, Document Manager, Project Manager and Workspaces. And this is all for free for first year. After first year there is an annual fee for USD 14.95.

This offer is great if you are planning to get a business or a personal site up and running without any cost for the first year. Even if you already have a site, the offerings from Microsoft are still worth consideration.
